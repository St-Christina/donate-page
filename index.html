No Cost Giving

The following programs benefit our parish at no extra cost:

Escrip: Visit www.escrip.com, indicate that you wish to support St. Christina Orthodox Church, and register your debit/credit cards. Applies to participating stores (both brick & mortar and online).

GoodSearch & GoodShop: Visit www.goodsearch.com and tell them you want to support St. Christina Orthodox Church. GoodSearch is a Yahoo-powered internet search engine that donates a penny each time you enter a search. Similarly, www.goodshop.com is a portal that, when used at your usual online stores, prompts the stores to donate a percentage to the parish.
